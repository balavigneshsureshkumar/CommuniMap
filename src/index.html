About CommuniMap

GALLANT (Glasgow as a Living Lab Accelerating Novel Transformation) is a University project aiming to design novel ways to adapt to climate change. The Community Collaboration workstream facilitates various community science projects including "Movement Co-Lab" exploring how people move through their communities.

CommuniMap is a digital tool that acts as a data repository for community science data, allowing users to:

  • Share how they travel ‚Äî by foot, wheels, or otherwise
  • Map their routes
  • Flag barriers (e.g. broken pavements, missing crossings)
  • Highlight routes that feel safe, joyful, or inspiring

Our Solution: Pet Avatar Gamification

How We Gamified CommuniMap

We implemented a pet avatar system that users can customize using points they gather from walking or cycling on their daily routes. The points represent their carbon offset from choosing to walk or cycle over taking private transportation.
